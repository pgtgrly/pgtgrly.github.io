1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed with privacy as a core principle. The Service Provider collects minimal information:

  • Usage Data: No personal analytics or tracking behavior is collected by the Service Provider.
  • Device Data: The Application may access device capabilities (like Haptics and Notifications) but does not transmit this data externally.

2. Third Party Access

Only aggregated, anonymized data is transmitted to platform services required for the app's functionality (Apple iCloud). The Application uses:
