Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Data Collection

TaskBash is designed with privacy in mind.

  • Tasks & Data: All your tasks, settings, and habits are stored locally on your device and synced via your personal iCloud account. We (the developers) do not have access to your data.
  • Analytics: We do not collect personal analytics or track your usage behavior.

2. iCloud Sync

Your data is synced using Apple's CloudKit. The security and privacy of this data is governing by Apple's privacy policy and security standards.

3. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@taskbash.app.
